Title of research study

Public opinions of politicians

IRB Protocol Number

19-0183

Investigator

Tor Wager, PhD

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of the study is to help our team to better understand the public opinions towards the potential state senate candidates in the 2020 congressional election.
We expect that you will be in this research study for 30 minutes.
We expect about 750 people will be in this research study.

Explanation of Procedures

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be shown a series of photos of potential state senate candidates, and information about candidates such as news report about the candidates.
Your task is to rate how much you would support the candidates in the 2020 congressional election. You will also be asked to fill out some questionnaires about your demographic information and personal political ideology.
We cannot tell you everything about what we are doing in this study or why. A full explanation of the purpose of the research and procedures will be provided after you complete the study.

Voluntary Participation and Withdrawal

Whether or not you take part in this research is your choice. You can leave the research at any time and it will not be held against you.

Risks and Discomforts

There will be no potential harm to you.

Confidentiality

Information obtained about you for this study will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law. Research information that identifies you may be shared with the University of Colorado Boulder Institutional Review Board (IRB) and others who are responsible for ensuring compliance with laws and regulations related to research, including people on behalf of the Office for Human Research Protections. The information from this research may be published for scientific purposes; however, your identity will not be given out.

Payment for Participation

If you agree to take part in this research study, we will pay you $3 for your time and effort. Payments will be made through the MTurk platform after the aiming 750 participants data collection completed. However, you will not receive payment if you do not complete all experimental procedures.
It is important to know that payment for participation is taxable income.
